ABOUT

The department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 1994 and the department provides Post Graduate Programme in M.E Computer Science and Engineering. The department is accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi for the fourth time and has excellent infrastructure facilities with well experienced and well qualified faculty members. The Department signed MoUs with leading organizations such as Pricol Technologies Ltd., Paxterra for collaborative consultancy works.
